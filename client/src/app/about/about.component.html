About PeekNshop

Displaying of the products/inventory that are available in small businesses and local stores which could be far more affordable compared to the conglomerates. Also, with an added feature of freelance the delivery for those products.

Problem Statement

People often tend to go to a store to quickly grab a thing and get out, if it isn't available in that store, he/she have to drive down to another one. Whereas most international students wouldn't have private transport, therefore got to run around stores which in turn wastes their money and time. Our product can be devised as a solution that would let people set at their home and have a look at the live inventory right before heading to a store and get to know the live offers availed at other stores to compare prices and have products delivered at your door if you are lucky. Moreover, a person can get money for delivering something on his way back home. If a person goes to a shop to buy an item like insulation tape and the insulation tape is not available in the store, then the person could go to another store searching for that item like insulation tape using his car. But most international students don’t have a private means of transport, and they couldn’t waste their time travelling store to store in search of that item.

Solution

This is a problem initially faced by us as international students in a new place, we started with knowing whether this is a problem faced by not only a particular region but also people who are new to the place. We also got to know whether this would benefit the natives as well from any international students and concluded to implement several new features based on the respondent’s resonances. As a resident of a condominium, which is a cluster of diverse people, we started with questionnaires and surveys conducted internally and around the local area. We got to the local stores, getting to know how people feel 'what-if' our solution exists, we also got to know how it would help them. By extending our survey to international students in our college and even natives, through several interviews and google forms we enhanced our idea. Major user-centric features include live inventory, offers, and freelancing delivery which will help the international students to choose where to get affordable items that they are seeking even if they are new and not fond of the new place, they are in. Updating the list of items available from all the nearest stores and displaying them to the users so that they could grab their product right away from that store without having to move around. Displaying the live offers/ price markdowns would benefit both the customer as well as the store to attract more customers. Finally, a feature like freelancing delivery is an added asset that brings money to students who would like to pick up and drop someone's order on their way, and the other hand, for the customers who do not have to get to the store and get their order delivered within short periods.
